# Duolune - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

This document explains how Duolune collects, uses, maintains, and protects your personal information.

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for using Duolune (referred to as "the Application," "we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy describes our practices regarding data collection, usage, retention, protection, and your rights.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When using the Application, you may provide:

- Login credentials including identifier, display name, and profile image

- Optional profile information you choose to share

- Content you create, upload, or transmit through the Application

- Communications when seeking support or providing feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

For proper functionality of the Application, we automatically collect certain technical information:

- Device model

- Operating system version

- Application version

- Network connection status

- Diagnostic logs

- Aggregate usage data including feature interaction patterns and navigation flows

This data helps us improve products, optimize performance, and resolve issues.

### 2.3 Permissions

The Application may request specific device permissions based on features you use.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services

- Enable features you request

- Personalize your experience

- Send important service communications

- Respond to inquiries and support requests

- Monitor performance, resolve issues, and enhance security

## 4. Data Storage and Sharing

### 4.1 Storage and Security

Data is stored on your device and/or on secure remote servers, depending on features used. Security measures include:

- Encrypted data transmission

- Protected storage systems

- Controlled access mechanisms

Please note that no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

### 4.2 Sharing Practices

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Limited disclosure may occur only:

- With service providers necessary for operations

- When required by law

- To protect legal rights, user safety, or platform integrity

## 5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable regulations and available features, you may:

- Access your stored information

- Correct or update your data

- Delete some or all of your data

- Download your data where supported

- Manage permissions through device settings

To request deletion of your account or cloud data, please contact us using the information below.

## 6. Guest Mode

When guest mode is available, certain features may function without account registration. In such cases, data remains on your device, though account-dependent features may be unavailable.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and older (or as required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without proper consent. If you believe a child has submitted information without consent, please contact us for corrective action.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Third-party integrations may include hosting, analytics, authentication, or notification services. These partners process minimal data per their own privacy and security commitments.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date reflects changes. Material changes may be announced through the Application or other channels.
